Policy & Guidelines

The Best Class Competition is a prestigious annual event designed to foster academic excellence, leadership skills, community responsibility, and career growth among students across all departments.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Academics & Performance: Classroom pass percentages, high academic grades, and academic improvements.
  • Skill Upgradation: NPTEL, Coursera, MOOCs, and external professional certifications completed by class students.
  • Professional Experience: Industry internships (both offline and online) and placement records.
  • Research & Innovations: Student publications, patent filings, and institutionally funded projects.
  • Social Responsibility: Direct active student participation in NCC, NSS, and local community outreach drives.

Verification Workflow

1

Submission

Students upload academic and co-curricular achievements with valid PDF certificates/proof.

2

Teacher Verification

Class Teachers review submissions, either verifying them or requesting edits/clarification.

3

Evaluation & Marks

The institutional Evaluation Team awards scores according to strict criteria parameters.

4

IQAC/HOD Monitoring

HODs and IQAC officers monitor real-time class metrics, stand-wise rankings, and analytics.
